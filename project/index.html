CHECKMATE

Checkmate started from a few basic diagrams and developed to complicate but organized plans, sections and facade. Typology is important in this studio. Checkmate is a new museum in MASS MoCA, MA.

One of the goals of the studio will be to couple together strategies for the architectural program and the organization of the buildings that enable it. Strategy implies a larger idea about organizing and relating the various parts that make up the whole (think of strategy in a game of chess). On the other hand, tactics suggest a careful reading of the particular and the local, seizing a more unique opportunity that emerges from the larger organization of the whole. As we begin to explore architectural strategies for adding to the campus, we will strive to align them with the programmatic consequences they provoke.
